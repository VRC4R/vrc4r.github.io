VRChat used to have the ability to host rooms with world files hosted on other web servers. A few of these “user generated worlds” are, at the time of writing, currently hosted on a seperate "vrc-worlds" S3 bucket.

All of these were sourced from this archived page:
	https://archive.fo/E1ti0
and are archived via the Wayback Machine and the Internet Archive.
